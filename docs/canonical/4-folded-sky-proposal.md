## Visitor flow

Per room spec doc 1 visitor route. One-way, no doubling back.

## Pathway (5–7 minutes)

- Foam mat floor underfoot. Shoes on.
- 10 flashlights at entry station, first come first served.
- Approximately 20 of 30 visitors enter without one.
- Flashlight body has small visible glow (so visitors can identify who is holding one).
- Wall panels with 4–5 firefly facts on painted stick-on letters along `back-wall` (X=0) and `window-wall` (Z=8.78) faces.
- LED strip along floor edge for wayfinding.
- Phones allowed in `pathway` ONLY. NOT in `forest`.
- Final panel before forest entry: soft hint about settling the beam.

## Forest experience – three stages

### Stage 1: flicker

- Duration: 1–3 minutes.
- Room already alive on group entry.
- Fireflies wake and fade across ceiling forms on their own.
- Distribution uneven – some forms dense with activity, some quiet.
- No visitor input. Group watches.

### Stage 2: interactive

- Duration: 6–8 minutes.
- Visitors with flashlights sweep beams at ceiling forms.
- Beam hits ceiling: pool of fireflies wakes, ~2–3m diameter.
- Beam moves: pool moves.
- Beam away: pool fades to background.
- Beam held still: pool densifies over time.
- 10 moving pools across the ceiling.
- Watchers cluster around holders in groups of 2–3.
- Pattern legibility: ~2–3 minutes for cause-and-effect to be readable.

### Stage 3: programmed movement

- Duration: 2–3 minutes.
- Trigger: approximately 70% of flashlights pointed down or held still.
- All ceiling forms pulse in unison over 2–3 minute build.
- Cross-ceiling programmed patterns possible – flows, zone transitions.
- Peak synchrony: ~30 seconds. The most coherent moment, NOT necessarily the brightest.
- Decoherence: ~1 minute.
- Distant thunder audio cue.
- Room dims.
- Group exits.

## Lighting (forest)

- Loofah wall (`front-wall`): hidden warm light behind bamboo armature. Per doc 3.
- All other wall surfaces: matte dark.
- Overhead spotlights at seating zones: dim, focused downward.
- `pathway` edge LED strips faintly leak into forest perimeter.

### Dropped from earlier versions

- Sundown wall colour progression (yellow → purple). Tied to wall lighting that no longer exists. The IDEA is kept as a possibility for ceiling lighting or synchrony stage timing, but the wall implementation is removed.
- Breath-paced wall pulse. Same reason.
