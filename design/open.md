# Open — what's still being decided

Nothing here is settled. These are live choices, unresolved conflicts, and things
waiting on real-world input. **Never treat anything on this page as decided.** When
one gets resolved, move it into [decisions.md](decisions.md) with the date and
delete it from here.

---

## Big creative choices (none picked yet)

- **Which experience storyline?** Three are on the table, all sharing the same
  room: *the room remembers* (surfaces hold a charge and glow into the next
  session), *I called one* (holding a flashlight summons a firefly to you), and
  *the forest doesn't need you* (flashlights scatter the fireflies like real
  animals). One will be chosen.
- **Loofah wall — which look?** A neat woven grid versus a looser, organic
  fibrous look. Kept as switchable options to compare; final pick deferred.

## Waiting on the real world

- **The interactive firefly trigger has never been prototyped.** The biggest
  unknown in the project — whether and how a flashlight can actually summon or
  scatter the fireflies. Blocks committing to any of the interactive storylines.
- **Mist install logistics.** The design is decided (floor-rising ground mist —
  see decisions). What's still open is the physical side: venue sign-off for smoke
  detectors and airflow, and the machine itself.
- **Loofah wall specifics** — final dimensions, exact colour, light intensity, and
  how it turns the corners — decided on install day.
- **Seating versus capacity.** The room must seat up to 30, but a recent bench
  idea (from one of the concept images) needs reconciling with that number.
- **Emergency exit and signage** — a venue safety-code question, not yet resolved.
