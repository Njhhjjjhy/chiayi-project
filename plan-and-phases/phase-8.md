# Phase 8: public promotional website (future)

## Goal

Wrap the final 3d experience in a public-facing website for promoting the physical installation. This phase is for the future, after the installation design is finalized.

## What to build

### 1. Landing page

- Project title and tagline.
- Brief description of the experience (what visitors will see and feel).
- Location: Alishan mountain range, Taiwan.
- Connection to Tsou indigenous culture and ecology (handled respectfully).
- Exhibition times and visitor info (placeholder content until details are confirmed).

### 2. Embedded 3d experience

- The final polished scene from phase 7, embedded in the page.
- Either scroll-driven (scrolling advances the timeline) or auto-playing on a loop.
- A "skip to fireflies" option for impatient visitors.
- Fallback for low-powered devices: a video capture or animated gif of the experience.

### 3. Responsive design

- Works on desktop and mobile.
- On mobile, the 3d scene may use a simplified version (fewer particles, lower resolution) for performance.
- Touch controls for mobile interaction.

### 4. Audio integration

- Ambient nature sounds that shift with the phases (birdsong in golden hour, crickets in twilight, silence in blue hour, subtle forest night sounds during fireflies).
- Audio starts muted with a clear "enable sound" prompt.
- Placeholder audio files initially; real recordings from Alishan later.

### 5. Seo and sharing

- Open graph metadata for social sharing.
- A compelling preview image (screenshot of the golden hour or firefly phase).
- Proper title and description tags.

### 6. Production deployment

- Vercel production deployment with a custom domain (tbd).
- Performance optimization for fast initial load.
- Asset compression and lazy loading.

## Deliverable

A live promotional website at a public url, showcasing the firefly immersive experience.

## Acceptance criteria

- Landing page loads quickly and looks polished.
- 3d experience runs smoothly on desktop.
- Mobile fallback works.
- Audio is present but starts muted.
- Social sharing metadata produces a good preview.
- Deployed to Vercel and accessible via url.
